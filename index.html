Project Overview

Objective: To design, configure, and pilot a secure Microsoft Intune mobile device management (MDM) environment that meets Ontario nuclear plant security requirements for TSSA's nuclear inspectors — while establishing the foundation for a broader organizational migration from AirWatch to Intune.

Current State
TSSA manages mobile devices through AirWatch (VMware Workspace ONE), with ~300 staff across the organization.
Urgent Requirement
Ontario nuclear plants mandate strict on-site device controls: camera disablement, hotspot blocking, app restrictions, enforced security posture for ~15 nuclear inspectors.
Bruce Power Approval
Security controls must be reviewed and approved by Bruce Power security teams before any inspector brings a managed device on-site.
Phased Approach
POC with 2–3 inspectors, then production pilot up to 30 users (inspectors + IT). Full AirWatch replacement (~300 staff) is a future engagement.

Success Criteria & KPIs

Security Compliance
Bruce Power–mandated restrictions (camera off, hotspot blocked, unauthorized app controls) enforced on all pilot devices.
Pilot Validation
POC (2–3 users) and production pilot (up to 30 users) completed with zero unresolved critical issues.
Configuration Stability
Pilot devices maintain consistent security posture with no compliance drift.
Defender Integration
Microsoft Defender for Endpoint onboarded on mobile devices with risk signals feeding Conditional Access.
Client Handoff
TSSA IT staff equipped to independently manage, monitor, and adjust the Intune environment.
Future-Ready Foundation
Environment documented to support broader AirWatch-to-Intune migration (~300 staff).

SCOPE REVIEW

What's In and What's Out

What's In Scope

Planning & Requirements
Formal documentation of Bruce Power–mandated restrictions, architecture definition.
Intune Environment Build
Enrollment, compliance policies, configuration profiles, Conditional Access, MAM/APP policies.
Defender for Endpoint
Mobile onboarding, jailbreak/root detection, risk-based Conditional Access.
Certificate-Based Auth
SCEP/PKCS for Wi-Fi, VPN, and app access (dependent on client PKI).
Apple Business Manager
ABM integration and iCloud governance controls.
POC & Production Pilot
2–3 inspector devices (POC) and up to 30 users (production pilot) enrolled and validated.
Knowledge Transfer
1–2 hour session plus full administrative runbook for TSSA IT staff.

What's Out of Scope

Full AirWatch Migration
Replacement for ~300 staff is a future engagement beyond this pilot.
PKI Infrastructure
A working PKI must be provided by TSSA. We will consume, not build, the infrastructure.
Network-Layer Filtering
No firewall, Netskope/SWG, or NAC configuration. Intune policies only.
Third-Party Integration
No integration with SIEM/EMM tools outside Intune ecosystem.
Extended Intune Support
No ongoing monitoring or proactive support after project closeout.
Helpdesk & End-User Support
No ticket resolution or general IT support outside the core pilot group.
